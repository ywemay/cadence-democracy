## 🔑 STEP 1: GET YOUR PHYSICAL VAULT PASSWORD
Frequency: Once every 6 months

   1. Show Up in Person: On registration day, walk directly to your designated local server barn or community meeting area.
   2. The Human Firewall: A panel of 10 to 20 randomly selected neighbors will verify your physical presence. No corporate algorithms or facial recognition clouds are used.
   3. Grab Your Paper Ticket: The automated USB printer will spool a physical receipt containing 5 unique, ultra-long alphanumeric password keys.
   4. Go Completely Offline: Once printed, these keys exist only on that piece of paper. The live database completely erases the raw text and saves only an un-hackable mathematical fingerprint (hash).

------------------------------
## 🗳️ STEP 2: VOTING ON THE WEEKLY RHYTHM
Frequency: Once a week at your own convenience

   1. Review Active Matters: Log onto the local network website from a phone, computer, or the shared barn terminal. Read the open proposals currently on the dashboard. [1] 
   2. Unlock the Vault: To cast a vote, you cannot use a simple login button. You must manually type all 5 keys from your paper ticket simultaneously into the form fields.
   3. The Elastic Clock Safety: Minor issues close fast (within 24 hours). If you see a major or corrupt issue gaining steam, don't panic—the moment participation climbs past 1%, the system automatically triggers a brake pedal, stretching the deadline out to a full week to guarantee the community has time to debate, investigate, and vote.
   4. Self-Selecting Wisdom: If an issue covers a highly technical topic (like complex soil biology) that you do not understand, you are encouraged to simply skip the vote. The system calculates the winner purely based on the ratio of active participants, ensuring society is guided by those who genuinely care and understand the issue.

------------------------------
## 👥 STEP 3: EMERGENCY SOCIAL RECOVERY
Use Case: If your paper ticket is lost, stolen, or taken by force

   1. Hit the Panic Button: Instantly log onto any terminal using your standard backup password to trigger an Account Freeze. This immediately bricks your 5 physical keys across the entire global network, rendering stolen papers completely useless.
   2. Gather Your 6 Vouchers: Find 6 registered citizens in your local neighborhood whom you know personally (friends, family, or neighbors).
   3. The Multi-Signature Handshake: Gather your 6 vouchers around a terminal. Each person must input one of their own active password keys to vouch for your physical identity.
   4. Instant Identity Regeneration: The moment the 6th neighbor signs, the server wipes your compromised credentials and prints a brand-new 5-key ticket. Because a single key can only be used for recovery once every 6 months, your neighbors' trust is a valuable shield that cannot be farmed by corporate bosses or criminal cartels.

------------------------------
## 🌾 STEP 4: THE ULTIMATE DEFENSE (FORKING THE BARN)
Use Case: If physical tyrants, police, or corrupt gangs occupy your server barn

   1. Walk Away: Do not engage in physical violence. Simply stop logging into the captured server terminal.
   2. Start Fresh Down the Road: Take a copy of the free, read-only open-source software code, install it on a cheap $50 micro-computer in a different barn, and hold a fresh registration day.
   3. Isolate the Tyrants: The global network grid will notice the original barn's data logs no longer match human consensus, automatically cutting it off. The tyrants will be left ruling an empty simulation with zero real users, bad internet reviews, and no power.
